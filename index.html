About

Allysson is a Brazilian software engineer. He currently lives in Burnaby, Canada, where he works for Maximizer as a senior software engineer.

His interest in programming started back in 2009 when he created his own blog about Naruto which taught him HTML, CSS, and a bit of PHP, he maintained the blog and a few others until 2012. Three years later, he entered a tech course at an university in his hometown, a couple of months later he was hired for his first tech job and since then he is deep diving into tech.

Fast-forward to now, he became an experienced professional with strong knowledge of C# and .NET and has hands-on experience with microservices, distributed systems, containers, event sourcing, DDD, CQRS, continuous integration, continuous delivery, and cloud technologies.

He is currently focusing his learnings and practices on software architecture, scalability, high performance, and design patterns for enterprise applications.
